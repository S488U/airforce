INDIAN NAVY

The Indian Navy is the maritime branch of the Indian Armed Forces. It is a blue-water navy that operates in the Persian Gulf region, the Horn of Africa to the Strait of Malacca. The President of India is the Supreme Commander of the Indian Navy, and the Chief of Naval Staff, a four-star admiral, commands the navy. The primary objective of the Indian Navy is to safeguard the country's maritime borders.
